## Request Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Eligibility">
    Verify software availability for your role/department
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Complete software request form with: - Software name and version - Business
    justification - Installation timeline
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approval">Wait for department and IT approval</Step>
  <Step title="Installation">Schedule installation or receive license key</Step>
</Steps>
