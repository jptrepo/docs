## Checkout Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Availability">
    Browse available equipment in the inventory system
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Complete the equipment request form with: - Checkout period - Purpose of use
    - Department code
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pickup Equipment">
    Visit the IT Service Desk during business hours
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return">Return equipment clean and in working condition</Step>
</Steps>

## Policies

<Note>
### Checkout Duration
- Standard: 2 weeks
- Extended: Up to 1 semester (requires approval)
- Rush: 24-48 hours

### User Responsibilities

- Report any damage immediately
- Return on time
- Do not install unauthorized software

</Note>
