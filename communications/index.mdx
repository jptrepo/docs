# Communications

The UVA Engineering Communications team supports the School's mission by telling our story through strategic communications, marketing, and media relations. We help faculty, staff, and departments share their achievements and maintain consistent messaging across all platforms.

## Our Mission

Our award-winning team of communications, marketing, graphic design, web, and public relations professionals focuses on strengthening UVA Engineering's brand identity and strategic messaging across all platforms and initiatives. We support faculty research announcements, student achievements, and departmental communications while ensuring alignment with UVA's institutional messaging.
