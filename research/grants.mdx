## Application Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Consultation">
    Meet with Research Development Office to discuss funding strategy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proposal Development">
    Work with grants team on: - Budget development - Project timeline - Required
    documentation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Internal Review">
    Submit for departmental and college review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submission">
    Final proposal submission through sponsored programs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
