# Purchasing Guidelines

## Request Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Budget">
    Verify available funds in your department budget.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Quotes">
    Obtain required number of quotes: - Under $5,000: One quote -
    $5,000-$25,000: Two quotes - Over $25,000: Three quotes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Request">
    Complete purchase request form with: - Item specifications - Vendor
    information - Budget codes - Required approvals
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Status">
    Monitor request status through the purchasing portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Policies

<Note>
### Approval Thresholds
- Department Head: Up to $5,000
- Dean: $5,000-$25,000
- VP Level: Over $25,000

### Timeline

- Standard purchases: 5-7 business days
- Complex/High-value: 2-4 weeks
- Emergency: 24-48 hours

</Note>
